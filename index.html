Our Vision

Our Vision

A metaverse-wide network of NFTs created by planting Forests!

We are using the nature as our guide and recreate its genious using Web3 technology.
In our metaverse, we see the blockchains as the foundation - the Forest ground, and NFTs are the data units - the Trees. We develop the means to create the links - the Mycelia, to connect those trees, support them and transport the information from one tree to another.

Our goal is to see this metaverse as an open, connected, fair and prosperous space for all metaverse citizens.
