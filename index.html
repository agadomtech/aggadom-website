Vision

Our goal is to create a world where any idea, knowledge, achievement and asset are open, accessible and appriciated. A world where individual and collaborative creativity is deeply-rooted and empowered. A world where people can openly share ideas and creations and be rewarded for inspiring and enabling others to create and achieve.
A world where open source is for anything and the contributors are properly rewarded.

Currently, the major portion of human creativity is still highly influenced by the old economic models. The driving force of those models is competition.

By developing new economic and financial incentives model as the means for mutually beneficial collaborations, harnessing each individual’s personal strengths and passion, we can truly unleash humanity’s potential.

Mission

We are developing the protocol which is the technological infrastructure to enable

  • NFT Platforms to interact
  • NFT creators to semantically connect their NFTs with other, existing NFTs
  • NFT creators to accumulate layers of value, creating one NFT on top of another
  • to represent continuously evolving NFTs connected semantically to be
    both the origins and the sources of inspiration

This protocol unlocks an ocean of use cases that were not possible before.
