Vision

Our goal is to create a world where any idea, knowledge, achievement and asset are valuable and accessible. A world where individual and collaborative creativity is deeply-rooted and empowered. A world where people are attributed and rewarded for inspiring and enabling others to create and achieve.

Currently, the major portion of human creativity is still highly influenced by the old economic models. The driving force of those models is competition.

By developing the means for mutually beneficial collaborations, harnessing each individual’s personal strengths and passion, we can truly unleash humanity’s potential.

Mission

We are building the technological infrastructure and community to enable and empower everyone to plant seeds of knowledge and to build and create upon seeds of others. Our protocol ensures a highly efficient semantic connection between records, and guarantees gaining financial value to highly valuable seeds of knowledge. By this we aim to inspire and indulge both humanity’s collaborative progress and each individual’s self-realization.

Principles

Nature is our role model and its creations are our inspiration
Creativity of any kind is valuable
Opinion of value is personal, but is unanimously respected
Recognition and appreciation for contributions and inspiration are inherent
Win-win collaborations are fundamental and essential
